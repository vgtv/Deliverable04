About Able

  
 

Today around one in ten people in the world live with a disability.  They are the largest minority in the world! REF

  

We at The Able Team wanted to do something that could help people with a disability. Something that could make everyday life easier for this big minority group. After a lot of discussing on how we could do that, we came up with the idea of making Able.
We took contact with Oslo Kommune to asked if they wanted to cooperate with us on this project. (Så her tenkte jeg vi kunne finne en måte å referere til emailen)

One of the main issues for people with a physical disability are accessibility.  They can face innumerable physical obstacles and barriers every day.  Snow, stairs, curbs, a small sidewalk, public transportation and lack of accessible information are a  few examples.   Acknowledging that there are many things in our society people without disabilities take for granted, we  have, in cooperation with Oslo Kommune, developed the mobile application Able.

  

With Able, disabled people can take an active part in notifying Oslo Kommune and fellow citizens about  any physical barrier they may encounter in Oslo.

   

Our vision is to make life for people with disabilities as easy as it is to others. Disabled people should not dread to go out becuse their afraid to meet obstructions. We hope this application will be usefull, and help you in everyday life. If it is some features you miss, please help us make the app better, and send us an email!
